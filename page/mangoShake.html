Mango Shake (Detailed)

Ingredients

  • Ripe mango – 1 large
  • Cold milk – 1 cup
  • Sugar – 1 to 2 tablespoons (optional)
  • Ice cubes – 4–5 (optional)

Step-by-Step Method

  1. Prepare mango
  2. Wash, peel, and chop mango into pieces.
  3. Remove the seed.
  4. Blend
  5. Add mango pieces to blender.
  6. Add milk, sugar, and ice cubes.
  7. Blend until smooth
  8. Blend for 30–40 seconds until creamy.
  9. Serve
  10. Pour into a glass and serve immediately.

✨ Optional add-ons:

1 scoop vanilla ice cream

A pinch of cardamom powder

Chopped dry fruits on top
